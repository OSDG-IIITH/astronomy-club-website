We are one of the student clubs at IIIT-Hyderabad. We are Astronomy enthusiasts who love to stargaze and also educate others about it.

Activities

Stargazing Nights

We conduct weekly stargazing sessions atop Tarangini(Amphitheatre) or Himalayan Amphitheatre

Star Party

We travel to low light polluted areas to enjoy a night of stargazing and photography.

Informative Sessions

We conduct these sessions to familiarize people with the latest updates in astronomy and related fields.
